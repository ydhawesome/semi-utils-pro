Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Framely online watermark tool ("the Service") handles your data.

Photos you upload

Photos you upload are used solely to generate the watermarked result you requested. They are stored temporarily on the server only for the duration of processing and are not used for any other purpose, shared, or sold. Temporary files are removed automatically by the hosting platform.

Processing in your browser

Large images may be resized in your browser before upload. This happens entirely on your device.

Cookies and analytics

The Service may use cookies and third-party services (such as analytics and advertising providers, including Google) to operate the site and display ads. These third parties may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising via your browser settings or your ad provider's preferences.

Language preference

Your selected language is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and is not transmitted to the server.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the site operator.
